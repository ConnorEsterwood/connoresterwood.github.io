Research

I focus my studies on the relationships between humans and machines. In particular, I study the effect of trust and trust repair.

In short, robots will make mistakes eventually and in doing so will jeopardize the trust that human’s have in them. How do they recover form this violation and what is the best way to do so? Does it make a difference if they look or act human? Does the type of violation matter? These are all questions I’m interested in and examine in my past and present research stream. Below are some specific projects I have been involved in in the last few years.

Trust Repair & Trustworthiness

Personality, Acceptance, & Trust

Situational Awareness, Trust Repair & Multi-Agent Teaming

Autonomous Vehicles, Trust, & Acceptance
